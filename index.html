FAQ

What is Heallense?

Heallense is a browser-based AI wellness assistant that helps users understand health conditions, track daily habits, and get trusted health insights — all without installing an app.

Is Heallense a medical app or a doctor replacement?

No. Heallense is not a replacement for doctors or medical professionals. It provides educational health information and encourages users to consult qualified healthcare providers when needed.

How does the AI in Heallense work?

Heallense AI retrieves information from trusted medical sources (such as NHS, WHO, and MedlinePlus) and answers only when verified data is available - It does not guess or hallucinate.

Does Heallense suggest medicines?

No, Heallense may explain what certain medicines are commonly used for, but it never prescribes medications or provides dosages.

Is my health data safe?

Yes. User data is securely stored using Firebase with proper authentication and access rules. Heallense follows a privacy-first approach and does not sell or misuse user data.

Who is Heallense for?

Heallense is designed for: Students managing stress and routines, Working professionals dealing with burnout, Anyone looking for simple, reliable wellness guidance

Do I need to install an app to use Heallense?

No. Heallense works entirely in the browser, making it lightweight, fast, and accessible on any device.

Is Heallense free to use?

Yes, Heallense is completely free to use. No hidden fees or subscriptions are required.

Does Heallense send reminders automatically?

Heallense can enable smart reminders by default (like hydration or breathing breaks), which users can customize or turn off anytime
